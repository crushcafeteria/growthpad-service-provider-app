Buy Growthpad credits
Credits allow you to receive orders from customers in Growthpad platform
1. On the M-PESA menu, select Lipa na MPESA 2. Choose Buy Goods And Services 3. Enter till number {{config.mpesa.till_no}} 4. Enter the amount you wish to pay 5. Enter your PIN and confirm

Ksh 1 = 1 credit
Each order you recieve = 10 credits
